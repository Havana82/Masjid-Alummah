About Us

Masjid Al-Ummah was established in 2015 in Huntsville, Texas out of the need for an Islamic Center where the Muslim community can perform their religous obligations, organize Islamic historic events, and pass on true Islamic beliefs and traditions to our next generations. We initially started in a rented 500 square feet suite, and Alhamdulillah by the middle of 2016, the Society secured a property within the vicinity to fulfill these needs. The current physical location is 3516, Montgomery Rd, Huntsville, Texas 77340 which is centrally located within Huntsville. There are separate prayer halls for men and women, clean restroom areas, and outside play area. The society enjoys a varierty of programs all year round. We are committed to serving the local Muslim community, by providing dedicated and enlightening sessions. Our mission is to cultivate a deeper connection with Allah through the beauty of Salat and fostering a sense of peace and spirituality for all who enter our sacred space.
Masjid Al-Ummah enjoys Non-profit Tax exempt status under IRS (501 C).
